Hot and Spicy Indomie

Time to prepare: ~3 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️

Description

Hot and Spicy-flavored Indomie Instant Noodles is a fiery and bold variation of the iconic Indomie Mi Goreng series. Designed for spice lovers, this flavor takes the classic dry-style instant noodles and turns up the heat, delivering a thrilling and satisfying experience for those who crave a kick of spice in their meals. The dish features Indomie’s signature thin, springy noodles, which are cooked and then tossed in a spicy seasoning blend. The result is a flavorful, spicy, and slightly sweet noodle dish with a satisfying crunch from the fried shallots. The heat is prominent but balanced by the sweetness of the soy sauce, making it an addictive and enjoyable meal for spice enthusiasts. Hot and Spicy-flavored Indomie is versatile and can be enjoyed as is or customized with additional toppings like fried eggs, vegetables, or protein to make it a more substantial meal. It’s a quick and easy option for those who love bold, spicy flavors and want to add some excitement to their instant noodle experience. This flavor is perfect for anyone who enjoys a fiery kick in their food.

Instructions

Boil the Noodles:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • Add the Indomie Mi Goreng noodles
  • Cook for a maximum of 3 minutes on medium heat

Prepare the Sauce:

  • On a plate, combine the sesame oil, chili powder, seasoning powder, and soya sauce provided in each noodle pack.
  • Mix the sauces and the noodles together (Optional)
  • You are now ready to serve!
