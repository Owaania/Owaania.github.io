Original Indomie

Time to prepare: ~3 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Description

Original Indomie Mi Goreng is a legendary instant noodle that has captured the hearts of millions worldwide. Known for its simplicity, versatility, and irresistible flavor, it’s a staple in many households and a beloved comfort food. The dish features thin, springy noodles that are cooked and then tossed in a flavorful blend of seasonings. Unlike soup-based instant noodles, Indomie Mi Goreng is a dry stir-fried style, making it unique and highly customizable. The result is a harmonious blend of sweet, savory, and slightly spicy flavors with a hint of smokiness from the fried shallots. The noodles are perfectly chewy, and the seasoning clings to them, creating a satisfying bite every time. Original Indomie Mi Goreng is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed as is or customized with additional toppings like fried eggs, vegetables, or protein. It’s a quick, affordable, and delicious meal that appeals to a wide range of tastes. This flavor is a timeless classic and a global favorite, loved for its simplicity, bold flavors, and comforting appeal.

Instructions

Boil the Noodles:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • Add the Indomie Mi Goreng noodles
  • Cook for a maximum of 3 minutes on medium heat

Prepare the Sauce:

  • On a plate, combine the sesame oil, chili powder, seasoning powder, and soya sauce provided in each noodle pack.
  • Mix the sauces and the noodles together (Optional)
  • You are now ready to serve!
