Original Buldak Ramen

Time to prepare: ~5 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Desecription

Original Buldak Ramen is the iconic and fiery instant noodle that started the Buldak Bokkeummyun (Fire Chicken Noodles) craze. Known for its intense spiciness and bold flavors, it has become a global favorite among spice lovers and instant noodle enthusiasts. The dish features thick, chewy noodles coated in a glossy, sticky sauce that packs a powerful punch. The sauce is a perfect blend of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors, with the heat level being the star of the show. The spiciness comes from a combination of chili peppers and other spices, delivering a fiery kick that lingers on the palate. Despite the heat, the sauce has a slightly sweet undertone, which adds balance and depth to the flavor profile. Original Buldak Ramen is often enjoyed as a stir-fried noodle dish, where the noodles are cooked and then tossed in the spicy sauce until fully coated. Some versions include a small packet of roasted sesame seeds or seaweed flakes to add a nutty or umami touch, enhancing the overall experience. This flavor is not for the faint of heart—it’s designed for those who crave extreme heat and bold flavors. It’s a go-to choice for spice enthusiasts and has gained a cult following for its ability to deliver a thrilling and satisfying eating experience. Original Buldak Ramen is a true testament to the love of spicy food in Korean cuisine and remains a staple in the world of instant noodles.

Instructions

  • Boil water: Heat a pot of water to boiling.
  • Add noodles: Put the Buldak noodles in the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions, but slightly undercook them.
  • Drain most of the water: Remove most of the water, leaving a small amount (around 2-3 tablespoons) in the pot.
  • Add sauce: Stir in the sauce packet from the package.
  • Stir-fry: Continue stirring until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the noodles.
  • Top with desired toppings: Add your favorite toppings like sesame seeds, chopped scallions, or cheese.
