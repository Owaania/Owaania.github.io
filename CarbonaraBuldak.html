Carbonara Buldak Ramen

Time to prepare: ~5 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️

DESCRIPTION

Carbonara-flavored Buldak Ramen is a unique and indulgent twist on the iconic Korean spicy instant noodle, Buldak Bokkeummyun (Fire Chicken Noodles). This flavor combines the fiery heat of the original Buldak Ramen with a creamy, cheesy carbonara sauce, creating a rich and decadent fusion of Korean and Italian flavors. The dish features thick, chewy noodles coated in a creamy, slightly sweet, and cheesy sauce that balances the intense spiciness of the Buldak seasoning. The carbonara element adds a layer of richness with its buttery, cheesy, and slightly smoky notes, making it a favorite for those who enjoy bold and complex flavors. The heat level is still significant, as it retains the signature fiery kick of the original Buldak Ramen, but the creaminess of the carbonara sauce helps to mellow the spice slightly. Toppings often include a packet of dried cheese powder or flakes, which enhance the creamy texture and add an extra layer of umami. Some versions may also include small bits of vegetables or protein for added depth. Carbonara-flavored Buldak Ramen is perfect for adventurous eaters who love the combination of spicy and creamy flavors. It’s a popular choice for those seeking a comforting yet thrilling instant noodle experience.

Instructions

Boil the Noodles:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • Add the Indomie Mi Goreng noodles
  • Cook for a maximum of 3 minutes on medium heat

Prepare the Sauce:

  • On a plate, combine the sesame oil, chili powder, seasoning powder, and soya sauce provided in each noodle pack.
  • Mix the sauces and the noodles together (Optional)
  • You are now ready to serve!
