Barbeque Chicken
Indomie

Time to prepare: ~5 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️

DESCRIPTION

Indomie Mi Goreng Hot & Spicy offers the perfect combination of spices, chili, and crispy fried onion that will awake all your senses and add sizzle to your palate!

Instructions

Boil the Noodles:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • Add the Indomie Mi Goreng noodles
  • Cook for a maximum of 3 minutes on medium heat

Prepare the Sauce:

  • On a plate, combine the sesame oil, chili powder, seasoning powder, and soya sauce provided in each noodle pack.
  • Mix the sauces and the noodles together (Optional)
  • You are now ready to serve!
