Barbeque Chicken Indomie

Time to prepare: ~3 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️

Description

Barbecue Chicken-flavored Indomie Instant Noodles is a smoky, savory, and slightly sweet variation of the beloved Indomie Mi Goreng series. This flavor takes inspiration from the rich, charred taste of barbecue chicken, offering a unique and satisfying twist on the classic dry-style instant noodles. The dish features Indomie’s signature thin, springy noodles, which are cooked and then tossed in a flavorful seasoning blend. The result is a deliciously smoky, savory, and slightly sweet noodle dish with a hint of spice and a satisfying crunch from the fried shallots. The noodles are perfectly coated in the flavorful sauce, making every bite a delightful mix of textures and tastes. Barbecue Chicken-flavored Indomie is versatile and can be enjoyed as is or customized with additional toppings like grilled chicken, vegetables, or a fried egg for extra protein. It’s a quick and easy meal that brings the comforting flavors of barbecue to your plate in minutes. This flavor is perfect for those who enjoy smoky, savory dishes with a touch of sweetness.

Instructions

Boil the Noodles:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • Add the Indomie Mi Goreng noodles
  • Cook for a maximum of 3 minutes on medium heat

Prepare the Sauce:

  • On a plate, combine the sesame oil, chili powder, seasoning powder, and soya sauce provided in each noodle pack.
  • Mix the sauces and the noodles together (Optional)
  • You are now ready to serve!
