Cheese Buldak Ramen

Time to prepare: ~5 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Desecription

Cheese-flavored Buldak Ramen is a deliciously bold and creamy variation of the famous Korean spicy instant noodle, Buldak Bokkeummyun (Fire Chicken Noodles). This flavor takes the intense heat of the original Buldak Ramen and combines it with a rich, cheesy twist, creating a perfect balance of spiciness and creamy indulgence. The dish features thick, chewy noodles coated in a fiery, sweet, and savory sauce, enhanced by the addition of a cheese seasoning packet. The cheese flavor adds a smooth, creamy, and slightly tangy element that complements the intense spiciness, creating a satisfying contrast. The heat level remains high, as it stays true to the signature fiery kick of Buldak Ramen, but the cheese helps to mellow the spice slightly, making it more approachable for those who enjoy a creamy texture with their heat. Some versions of Cheese Buldak Ramen include a powdered cheese packet or even small cheese flakes that melt into the noodles, adding an extra layer of richness and umami. The result is a decadent, flavorful dish that’s both spicy and comforting. Cheese-flavored Buldak Ramen is a favorite among fans of Korean instant noodles who love the combination of spicy and cheesy flavors. It’s perfect for those looking to elevate their instant noodle experience with a creamy, indulgent twist while still enjoying the fiery thrill of Buldak Ramen.

Instructions

  • Boil water: Heat a pot of water to boiling.
  • Add noodles: Put the Buldak noodles in the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions, but slightly undercook them.
  • Drain most of the water: Remove most of the water, leaving a small amount (around 2-3 tablespoons) in the pot.
  • Add sauce: Stir in the sauce packet from the package.
  • Stir-fry: Continue stirring until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the noodles.
  • Top with desired toppings: Add your favorite toppings like sesame seeds, chopped scallions, or cheese.
