Original Buldak Ramen

Time to prepare: ~5 minutes

Rating average:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Desecription

Quattro Cheese-flavored Buldak Ramen is a rich and indulgent variation of the iconic Buldak Bokkeummyun (Fire Chicken Noodles). This flavor takes the intense spiciness of the original Buldak Ramen and combines it with the creamy, savory goodness of four different cheeses, creating a luxurious and satisfying instant noodle experience. The dish features thick, chewy noodles coated in a fiery, sweet, and savory Buldak sauce, elevated by the addition of a rich cheese seasoning blend. The "quattro cheese" (four cheeses) typically includes a mix of cheddar, mozzarella, cream cheese, and gouda, which melt into the noodles to create a creamy, cheesy, and slightly tangy flavor. The cheese not only adds a layer of richness but also helps to mellow the intense spiciness, making it more approachable for those who enjoy a balance of heat and creaminess. Quattro Cheese Buldak Ramen often comes with a powdered cheese packet or cheese sauce that blends seamlessly into the noodles, creating a gooey, decadent texture. The result is a dish that’s both spicy and comforting, with a perfect harmony of bold heat and cheesy indulgence. This flavor is a favorite among fans of Buldak Ramen who love the combination of spicy and cheesy flavors. It’s perfect for those looking to enjoy the fiery thrill of Buldak Ramen while indulging in a creamy, cheesy twist.

Instructions

  • Boil water: Heat a pot of water to boiling.
  • Add noodles: Put the Buldak noodles in the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions, but slightly undercook them.
  • Drain most of the water: Remove most of the water, leaving a small amount (around 2-3 tablespoons) in the pot.
  • Add sauce: Stir in the sauce packet from the package.
  • Stir-fry: Continue stirring until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the noodles.
  • Top with desired toppings: Add your favorite toppings like sesame seeds, chopped scallions, or cheese.
